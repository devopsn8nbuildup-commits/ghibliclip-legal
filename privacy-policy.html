Privacy Policy — GhibliClip

Last updated: 22 June 2026

GhibliClip ("we", "our", "the app") is an internal automation tool that generates and publishes short-form video content to our own social media accounts (YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Instagram Reels). This Privacy Policy explains what data the app processes and how.

1. What this app does

GhibliClip automatically generates short videos using AI (script generation, text-to-speech, AI video generation) and publishes them to social media accounts that we own and control. The app does not collect data from end users who view the published videos.

2. Data we process

  • API credentials (stored locally, never shared with third parties) used to authenticate with TikTok, YouTube, ElevenLabs, Anthropic, Kling AI, and Minimax APIs.
  • Content we generate ourselves (scripts, audio, video files) — stored locally on our own server.
  • Public engagement metrics (views, likes) of videos we publish, retrieved via each platform's official API for our own analytics dashboard.

3. Data we do NOT collect

We do not collect personal data from viewers, do not use cookies/trackers on any public-facing page, and do not share any data with third parties beyond the AI/social media APIs strictly required to operate the pipeline.

4. Third-party services used

  • TikTok Content Posting API — to publish video content to our own TikTok account.
  • YouTube Data API v3 — to publish video content to our own YouTube channel.
  • Anthropic Claude API — to generate video scripts.
  • ElevenLabs API — to generate voice narration.
  • Kling AI / Minimax API — to generate AI video visuals.

5. Data retention

Generated content and logs are stored on our own private server and retained as needed for operational purposes. API tokens are stored securely and are never published or shared.
